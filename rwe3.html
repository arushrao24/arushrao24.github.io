Formula SAE Rear Wing 3rd Element

Hytech Racing (FSAE EV)

Overview

  • Design and optimize aerodynamic components for track performance
  • Goal is to generate more downforce without significant increase in drag
  • The car's rear wing consists of 2 elements and generates ~31% of the car's total downforce

My Role

  • Used CATIA and Ansys Fluent to evaluate the feasbility of adding a 3rd element to the rear wing in a biplane configuration
  • Tested multiple airfoils and iterated through angles of attack (AoA)
  • Tested various positions for element (looked at multi-wing interaction)
  • Investigated other geometries like lofted wings, and adding a gurney
  • Finally decided that a 3rd element was not feasible due to a negligible performance improvement, mass gain, and manufacturing complexity
